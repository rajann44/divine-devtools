Love the internet again

Break free from big tech — our products put you in control of a safer, more private internet experience.

  • Firefox: Get the gold standard for browsing with speed, privacy and control.
  • Thunderbird: Go chaos-free with one app for all your emails, calendars and contacts.
  • Mozilla VPN: Keep your location and online adventures private — stream like a local, anywhere.
  • Mozilla Monitor: Get a heads-up if your personal info is at risk and lock it down like a pro.
  • Firefox Relay: Mask your email and phone number so you only get the messages you want.
  • Solo: Instantly create a beautiful website so you can grow your business.
  • 0DIN: Secure your AI by finding and fixing GenAI vulnerabilities before attackers do.
  • Tabstack: Reliably automate web tasks with a simple developer-focused API.
